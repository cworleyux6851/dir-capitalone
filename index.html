How I lead

I develop senior ICs and managers who own their decisions. The goal is a team that raises the bar with or without me in the room — that's what design excellence at scale actually requires.
Make the complex legible
Vision stories, journey maps, blueprints, prototypes — I use the right artifact for the right audience. A C-suite decision requires a different frame than an engineering handoff. I know the difference.

Not a feature to ship — a capability to build. At Citi, AI-driven design automated ~30% of routine servicing tasks. I'm continuing that work now in fintech, staying current on what's actually possible.
